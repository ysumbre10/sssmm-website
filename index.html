Welcome to Shree Swami Samarth Mitra Mandal

Founded in 2003, Shree Swami Samarth Mitra Mandal is dedicated to spreading devotion, community spirit, and cultural values through grand Ganesh Utsav celebrations and various social initiatives. Located at Sumbrenagar, Waki Khurd, Pune, our mandal brings devotees and volunteers together in harmony.

We organize bhajans, kirtans, health camps, blood donation drives, and many social activities throughout the year. Our mission is to strengthen community bonds and keep our rich traditions alive.

Explore our website to know more about our journey, dedicated members, memorable moments in our photo and video gallery, and how you can support and participate.
